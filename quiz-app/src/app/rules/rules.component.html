Rules & Regulations for the Online Quiz

  1. Quiz Format: Each question contains multiple-options based on the selected technology or topic.
  2. Time Limits:
    • 5 Questions – 5 minutes
    • 10 Questions – 10 minutes
    • 15 Questions – 15 minutes
  3. Automatic Submission: The quiz will be auto-submitted when time runs out.
  4. Single Attempt: Each question can only be attempted once.
  5. No Negative Marking: There is no penalty for wrong answers.
  6. Stable Internet: Ensure a stable internet connection throughout the quiz.
  7. Cheating Policy: Unfair means will lead to disqualification.
  8. Note: This is a practice test, so keep it clean by not cheating.
  9. Results: Results and analysis will be shown after quiz submission.

By starting the quiz, you agree to abide by these rules.
